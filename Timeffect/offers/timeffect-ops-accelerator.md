# Timeffect Ops Accelerator

## Who It’s For
High-touch builders, manufacturers, and operators (shipyards, marinas, prefab/modular housing, specialty fabrication) who rely on manual coordination and need digital systems that actually match their workflow.

## The Pain We Eliminate
- Inbox floods + lost leads when demand spikes.
- Zero visibility into time or cost on hand-built work.
- Frankenstacks (WordPress forms, spreadsheets, email) that require human babysitting.
- Quotes that take days because every configurator/estimate is rebuilt from scratch.

## The Promise
In 4–6 weeks we audit your workflow, design the exact tooling you need, and ship a production-ready ops system (booking, configurators, time tracking, dashboards) that your team actually uses. Productivity lift, quoting accuracy, and faster cash in the door—without hiring a dev team.

## Engagement Structure
1. **Recon & Blueprint (Week 1)**
   - Stakeholder interviews, systems audit, metric map.
   - Output: Ops Blueprint (flows, data model, success metrics).
2. **Foundations Sprint (Week 2–3)**
   - Build core modules (e.g., booking portal, configurator, time tracker) using modern stacks + AI-assisted dev to move fast.
   - Weekly reviews, sandbox access.
3. **Integration & Launch (Week 4–5)**
   - Hook into existing data sources (ERP, spreadsheets, email, SMS).
   - QA with your team, training assets, documentation.
4. **Stabilize & Scale (Week 6)**
   - Instrumentation (analytics, dashboards), backlog grooming, next-sprint roadmap.

## Deliverables (Customize per client)

- Time & cost tracking tied to projects and crews.
