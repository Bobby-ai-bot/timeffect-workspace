## Sales Script (Discovery Call)

**Opening:**  
"Thanks for your interest. I'm building a time-tracker for Muvor, a composite tooling shop in Estonia. They were struggling to price custom molds because labor hours were unpredictable. Sound familiar?"

**Discovery Questions:**
1. How many workers do you have?
2. How do you currently track labor hours?
3. What happens when a job goes over your labor estimate?
4. How do you price new custom work — gut feel or data?

**Close:**  
"Sounds like we can help. Next step is a €1k Blueprint where we map your workflow. If you proceed, that rolls into the build cost. Build takes 2-4 weeks, and early adopter pricing is €3k-€5k total. Want to move forward?"
