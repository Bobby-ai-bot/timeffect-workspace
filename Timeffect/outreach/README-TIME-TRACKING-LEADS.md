## Why These Companies?

**They all share the same problem:**
1. **Custom/variable work** → every job is different, hard to estimate labor
2. **Handwork-heavy** → can't automate, need human hours tracked accurately
3. **Profitability issues** → either losing bids (over-estimate) or losing money (under-estimate)

**They need:** Real-time labor tracking per job → accurate job costing → profitable quotes

## Outreach Strategy

### Messaging: Job-Costing Pain, Not "Time-Tracking SaaS"

**BAD:** "We have a time-tracking app with GPS and reports..."  
**GOOD:** "Are you guessing labor costs on jobs? We help fabricators price custom work accurately."

### Email Sequence:
1. **Email #1** (Day 1): "Are you guessing labor costs?" → introduce problem + solution
2. **Email #2** (Day 7): Follow-up → emphasize pain + simplicity
3. **Email #3** (Day 14): Final touch → case study + easy opt-in

Use templates:
- `email-time-tracking-1.md`
- `email-time-tracking-2.md`
- `email-time-tracking-3.md`

### Personalization Per Lead:
- **Baltic Workboats:** Reference shipyard case study directly ("We already built this for another Estonian shipyard...")
- **Metal shops (Metalcraft, Stalmex, Maru):** "Custom metalwork is impossible to price without real labor data..."
- **Field services (ProHandy, SmartFix):** "Are your techs logging hours accurately? Or guessing at end of day?"
- **Construction (Remondimees, Timbeco):** "Renovation jobs always go over budget because labor estimates are based on guesses..."
