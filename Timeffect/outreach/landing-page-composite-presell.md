## The Problem

### Are You Losing Money on Custom Work?

Most tooling and composite shops face the same problem:

- ❌ **Every job is unique** — no two molds or parts are the same
- ❌ **Labor estimates are guesses** — you don't know how long handwork actually takes
- ❌ **You find out too late** — job's already over budget by the time you realize it
- ❌ **Either lose bids or lose money** — over-estimate and competitors undercut you; under-estimate and you work for free

**Sound familiar?**

## The Solution

### Real-Time Labor Tracking for Custom Manufacturing

**Mobile App (iOS + Android):**
- Workers clock in/out per job (not per day)
- Simple tap-to-start interface
- Works offline (syncs later)

**Web Dashboard (for managers):**
- See who's working where in real-time
- Track labor hours per project
- Export job costing reports (actual vs. estimate)

**Result:**  
Know what jobs actually cost → price future work accurately → stay profitable
