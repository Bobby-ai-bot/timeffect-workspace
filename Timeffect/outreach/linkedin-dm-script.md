# LinkedIn DM Script

**Use case:** Warm-ish outreach to operations managers, founders, or tech leads at target companies

---

## Version 1: Direct Problem Call-Out

Hey [First Name] — saw you're running ops at [Company].

Quick question: does your team still manage [bookings/quotes/schedules] manually, or have you built something custom?

We just shipped a [booking portal / configurator / time tracker] for [similar company] in 2 weeks that eliminated [specific pain point]. Thought it might be relevant to [Company].

If you're open to a quick look, happy to share what we built. No sales pitch — just show and tell.

—Joosep | Timeffect

---

## Version 2: Mutual Interest Angle

[First Name] — noticed we're both in [industry / location / tech stack].

I build custom ops tools for manufacturers and operators (booking systems, configurators, time trackers). Just wrapped a project for [Company Name] that cut their quoting time by 80%.

Always curious how other companies in [industry] handle [specific workflow]. If you've got 15 min sometime, I'd love to compare notes.

—Joosep

---

## Version 3: Case Study Hook

Hey [First Name],

Wrote up a quick case study on a booking system we built for Tartu Turg (eliminated 95-email surge in 2 weeks). Thought you might find it relevant to [Company].

[Link to case study]

If you're dealing with anything similar — booking chaos, manual scheduling, inbox overload — happy to chat about what we could build for [Company].

—Joosep | Timeffect

---

## Follow-Up (if no response after 1 week)

[First Name] — following up on my last message.

Totally understand if timing isn't right. If you ever hit a wall with [specific pain], we specialize in fast custom builds (1-4 weeks). Happy to help when the time comes.

—Joosep

---

**Tips:**
- Keep it under 100 words
- Lead with their pain, not your solution
- Offer value (case study, quick look) before asking for call
- Mention Bobby if it fits — shows transparency + tech edge
