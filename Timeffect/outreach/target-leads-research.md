# Target Lead Research

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

**Firmographics:**
- Industry: Manufacturing, fabrication, marinas, venues, custom builders, service operators
- Size: 10-200 employees (small enough to need help, big enough to pay)
- Revenue: €1M-€50M (established but not enterprise)
- Location: Estonia, Nordics, EU (timezone + language overlap)

**Behavioral signals:**
- High-touch operations (manual coordination, custom work)
- Visible pain: slow quoting, booking chaos, no time tracking, spreadsheet hell
- Tech-forward enough to adopt digital tools but no in-house dev team

---

## Lead Categories & Research Sources

### 1. Marinas & Boatyards (Strong fit — booking + ops chaos)
**Why:** Seasonal demand spikes, manual booking, maintenance scheduling, slip management  
**Where to find:**
- Estonian Marinas Association member list
- Google: "marina Estonia" / "venemajutus Eesti"
- LinkedIn: Marina managers, harbor masters
- Example targets: Tallinn Yacht Club, Pirita Marina, Pärnu Yacht Club

### 2. Modular/Prefab Housing (Strong fit — configurators + quoting)
**Why:** Custom options, complex pricing, long quote cycles  
**Where to find:**
- Estonian Woodhouse Association
- Google: "modular house Estonia" / "moodulmaja tootja"
- LinkedIn: Founders at prefab/kit home companies
- Example targets: Palmatin, Harmet, Ecofin

### 3. Specialty Fabricators (Metalwork, Composites, Custom Manufacturing)
**Why:** Time tracking pain, quote accuracy, project visibility  
**Where to find:**
- Estonian Machine Industry Association
- Google: "custom fabrication Estonia" / "metallitöö kodumasin"
- LinkedIn: Operations managers at fabrication shops
- Example targets: Shipyards (Tallinn Shipyard, Baltic Workboats), metal shops

### 4. Event Venues & Marketplaces (Strong fit — booking systems)
**Why:** High email volume, scheduling conflicts, manual confirmations  
**Where to find:**
- Google: "event venue Tallinn" / "marketplace rentals"
- Similar to Tartu Turg (our case study)
- LinkedIn: Venue managers, operations leads

### 5. Rental Operations (Equipment, Tools, Vehicles)
**Why:** Booking chaos, inventory tracking, availability management  
**Where to find:**
- Google: "equipment rental Estonia" / "tööriista rent"
- Construction equipment, party rentals, specialty tools
- LinkedIn: Rental company owners/managers
